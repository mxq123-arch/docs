## Ask our docs

Use the keyboard shortcut Command + I (macOS) or Ctrl + I (Windows/Linux) to start a chat with our AI assistant trained on our documentation.

## Watch video tutorials

Visit our [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@GetMintlify/videos) channel for tutorials and guides on using Mintlify.

## Message support

Send us a message from your [dashboard](https://app.mintlify.com/). Click **Support** in the sidebar.

[![Contact support panel open in the dashboard.](https://github.com/mintlify/docs/raw/main/images/support-panel-light.png)](https://github.com/mintlify/docs/blob/main/images/support-panel-light.png)

[![Contact support panel open in the dashboard.](https://github.com/mintlify/docs/raw/main/images/support-panel-dark.png)](https://github.com/mintlify/docs/blob/main/images/support-panel-dark.png)

We aim to respond to all messages within 24 hours, but delays may occur during busy times.

## Contact via email

If you can't access your dashboard, email us at [support@mintlify.com](mailto:support@mintlify.com). Include the URL of your [dashboard](https://app.mintlify.com/) with your organization and deployment in your email so we can help you faster. For example, `app.mintlify.com/your-org/your-deployment`.
